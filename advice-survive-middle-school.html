How to Survive Middle School

Advice that my younger self who started Middle School and people starting Middle School should follow

  1. Ask for help
  2. Be yourself
  3. Everything will be just fine
  4. Do not Worry
  5. Don’t lean into peer pressure
  6. Say No to things you are uncomfortable with
  7. Be Nice and Friendly to Everybody around you
  8. Don’t get hung up to becoming popular / being popular
  9. if you are nice and friendly to everybody around you . You are and will be likeable enough already
  10. Go to the middle school before the first day to find where everything is and to relieve some anxiety about starting middle school with your parents
  11. Find your locker before the first day of middle school aka Grade 5
  12. Practice your locker combination
  13. Notice all your friends are in the same boat as you starting middle school as well metaphorically speaking not literally speaking
  14. Get to know your teachers
  15. Find a grade 9ner to show you around and be like your mentor to middle school. If you have any questions or worries to go to and to befriend as well
  16. Don’t stress about finding a boyfriend or girl friend . Just enjoy middle school single
  17. Focus on your grades . Dating can wait till maybe high school or later . Way later
  18. Get good grades and study hard
  19. Don’t try to fit in . When you are born to stand out
  20. Stay out of drama and trouble with friends
  21. If your friends turned toxic . Find new friends
  22. Look for the right people to befriend that won’t stab you on the back or gossip about you
